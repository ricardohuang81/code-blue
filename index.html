Blue

The color blue is all around us. Companies use it in their logos, Sports Teams sport it on their jerseys. It's in the sky, the ocean, and outer space. Our planet looks blue from afar due to the oceans. Animals are blue, fish are blue, movie characters, cartoon characters, our clothing, it even represents human emotions as we all feel "blue" at times. It is one of three primary colors of pigments in paiting and traditional color theory as well as in the RGB color model. Blue is mixed together with red and green to reproduce a plethora of other colors. It is truly an amazing color that has a significant impact on our everyday lives whether we realize it or not.
